Festivals and Culture of India

Bengali

World's sweetest language known for its literature, music, and the grand celebration of Durga puja.

Tamil

One of the world's oldest languages, with a proud tradition of classical dance, literature and temple art.

Punjabi

A poetic language that flourished under Mughal influence, known for its elegance, and literary depth.

Assamese

The cultural voice of Assam, rich in Bihu dance, classical literature, and lush riverine heritage.

Kashmiri

The language of the Heavenly Kashmir valley, known for its poetry, Sufi traditions, and serene charm.

Rajasthani

A group of colorful dialects echoing the grandeur of forts, folk tales, and desert festivals.

Malayalam

Spoken in Kerela, this language blends classical heritage with lush cultural expressions like Kathakali.
